# Privacy Policy for Social Sentry — Focus Mode

**Effective Date:** February 14, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Social Sentry — Focus Mode ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension handles your information.

## 2. Data Collection and Usage
**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

- **Local Processing:** All operations, including blocking distractions and managing settings, are performed locally on your device.
- **Settings Storage:** Your preferences (e.g., blocking options) are stored locally in your browser's storage (`chrome.storage.local`) and are never sent to external servers.
- **No Tracking:** We do not track your browsing history, collect usage analytics, or share data with third parties.

## 3. Permissions
The extension requires the following permissions to function:
- **Storage:** To save your preferences locally.
- **ActiveTab / WebNavigation:** To detect when you are on Facebook or YouTube and apply your focus settings (e.g., blocking shorts or feeds).
- **Host Permissions:** Access to `*.facebook.com` and `*.youtube.com` is required strictly to modify the page content (hide distractions) as per your settings.

## 4. Third-Party Services
This extension operates independently and does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services.
